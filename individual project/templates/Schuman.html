Background:

Born June 8th, 1810 in Zwickau, Saxony (Germany) and died July 29th, 1856 in Germany

German Romantic composer renowned particularly for his piano music, songs, and orchestral music

Many of his best-known piano pieces were written for his wife, the pianist Clara Schumann.
